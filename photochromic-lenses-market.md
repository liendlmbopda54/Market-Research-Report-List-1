<p><ul><li>Glass Photochromic Lenses</li><li>Standard Plastic (1.53 Coat and Uncoat)</li><li>Mid-Index Plastic (1.53 to 1.65)</li><li>High-Index Plastic (Above 1.65)</li><li>Polycarbonate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photochromic lenses market features several types based on material and refractive index. Glass photochromic lenses offer superior optical clarity but are heavier. Standard plastic lenses (1.53 coat and uncoat) are lightweight and affordable, while mid-index plastic lenses (1.53 to 1.65) provide a balance between thickness and weight. High-index plastic lenses (above 1.65) are designed for stronger prescriptions, offering thinner profiles. Polycarbonate lenses are impact-resistant and provide excellent protection, making them ideal for safety and sports eyewear.</p></p>
