<p><strong>The USB Keyboard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Keyboard</li><li>Membrane Keyboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The USB keyboard market is primarily divided into mechanical and membrane keyboards. Mechanical keyboards utilize individual mechanical switches for each key, offering tactile feedback, durability, and precision, making them popular among gamers and typists. In contrast, membrane keyboards use a pressure pad system with a rubber or silicone dome, providing a quieter typing experience but less tactile feedback. This distinction influences user preference, with mechanical keyboards often favored for performance and longevity, while membrane keyboards appeal for cost-effectiveness and portability.</p></p>
