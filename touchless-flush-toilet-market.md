<p><p>The touchless flush toilet market features two primary sensor types: radar sensors and infrared sensors. Radar sensors utilize microwave technology to detect user presence and trigger the flush, offering reliability even in various environmental conditions. In contrast, infrared sensors deploy light beams to detect motion, activating the flush when a user is detected nearby. Both technologies enhance hygiene and convenience by eliminating the need for physical contact, making them increasingly popular in public restrooms and modern home designs.</p></p>
